What's the best way to fight a bully?
Use their momentum against them.

The Westboro Baptist Church is a hate group, best known for their anti homosexual messages and protesting funerals. Most recently, they have announced they will be protesting the funerals of the victims of the Sandy Hook shootings to "praise God's judgement".

The first amendment protects the rights of groups like this, and they are entitled to their opinion. So are we, and we aim to do something about it. We will fight - but not by using hate. Instead we will use positive retribution. All donations to this cause will go to groups that the Westboro Baptist Church is protesting against, effectively using their momentum against them.
